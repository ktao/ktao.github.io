About

This site provides supplemental material and information about my PhD thesis entitled "Social Web Data Analytics: Relevancy, Redundancy, Diversity". I am currently writing and improving this thesis at Web Information Systems group, Delft University of Technology, the Netherlands, where I have been supervised by prof. dr. ir. Geert-Jan Houben.

Publications

The works in this thesis have been published in different venues relevant to the research area of information systems. The detailed list can be found at my homepage.

Datasets

We try our best to make the dataset that have been mentioned in the thesis publicly available for reproducing the results as well as enabling others to improve our methods.

  1. Relevance Estimation
    Dataset published on the supporting website for the paper entitled "Twinder: A Search Engine for Twitter Streams".
  2. Redundancy
    Dataset published on the supporting website for the paper entitled "Groundhog Day: Near Duplicate Detection on Twitter".
  3. Diversity
    Dataset published on the supporting website for the paper entitled "Building a Microblog Corpus for Search Result Diversification".
